Eco Fun Facts:
https://www.eesi.org/articles/view/data-centers-and-water-consumption
* The nation’s 5,426 data centers consume roughly 449 million gallons of
  water per day and 163.7 billion gallons annually (as of 2021).
https://usa.oceana.org/our-campaigns/plastic/
* An estimated 33 billion pounds of plastic enter the marine environment
  every year — that’s roughly equivalent to dumping two garbage trucks
  full of plastic into the oceans every minute.
https://www.un.org/en/exhibits/exhibit/in-images-plastic-forever
* Plastic waste can take anywhere from 20 to 500 years to decompose, and
  even then, it never fully disappears; it just gets smaller and smaller.
https://oceana.org/press-releases/report-switching-to-reusable-packaging-could-eliminate-1-trillion-single-use-plastic-bottles-and-cups/
* 10-percentage point increase in reusable beverage packaging by 2030
  could eliminate over 1 trillion single-use plastic bottles and cups and
  prevent up to 153 billion of these containers from entering our world’s
  oceans and waterways.
https://projectcleanwater.org/5-reasons-to-opt-for-reusable-water-bottles/
* The U.S. alone uses nearly 50 million plastic water bottles each year.
* Of the plastic water bottles purchased in the U.S., only 23% are recycled.
* It’s estimated that a $20 reusable water bottle will save you $6,180 in
  five years.
